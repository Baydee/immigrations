About Us

MEET CHRISTOPHER BENDER, RCIC CEO OF AZURVERDE IMMIGRATION

Trust, respect, and a commitment to excellence

I Understand the stress and uncertainty that clients face when immigrating to a new country. That is why I make it my mission to develop a high-quality client relationship to ensure a smooth process from start to finish. Having lived in various regions of Canada, I appreciate the advantages of growing up in Montreal, surrounded by a rich diversity of languages and cultures. I aim to help others achieve their dreams of coming to Canada. With over 15 years of experience in marketing and product development, I understand the importance of detail and timelines. As a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC R709171) recognized by the Québec Ministry of Immigration (#11981), I can submit immigration applications effectively. I speak fluent English and French, and I'm improving my Spanish daily. I look forward to helping you with your Canadian immigration needs.

A quick note to our valued visitors

If you are contacted by someone claiming that they are us or Christopher Bender, RCIC, on WhatsApp or Instagram, they are scammers. Please report them and do not send them money. Licensed immigration consultants will only ask for payment for a consultation or once we sign a service agreement with a client.

To avoid being scammed, beware of anybody who promises you a job in Canada or a guaranteed visa or work permit. And always verify a consultant’s information on the public register on the College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants website.
